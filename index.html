Ordering & Shipping

We accept orders starting from 5 units for bulk pricing. For smaller quantities, our standard pricing applies. Contact our sales team for custom arrangements.

Standard production lead time is 30-45 days depending on product and quantity. Shipping transit varies by destination: Asia 7-14 days, Europe 25-35 days, Americas 25-40 days. All shipments are tracked and insured.

Shipping is calculated per order based on destination, weight, and volume. We work with multiple logistics partners to offer competitive rates. Bulk and container orders qualify for preferential freight pricing. Contact us for a shipping estimate tailored to your order.

Product & Warranty

All Ningbo Siyang tools include a comprehensive 2-year manufacturer's warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Extended warranties are available.

Absolutely! Our entire product line is engineered for professional and industrial use. All tools meet or exceed industry standards including ISO 9001 certification.

Yes, we offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not completely satisfied, return the tool in original condition for a full refund or exchange.

Support & Maintenance

Yes, our 24/7 technical support team is available to help with product questions, troubleshooting, and maintenance guidance. Contact us via phone, email, or chat.

Each product comes with detailed maintenance instructions. We offer free maintenance training sessions and video tutorials on our learning center. Professional maintenance services are also available.

All genuine replacement parts are available and reasonably priced. We stock parts for 10+ years after product discontinuation to support long-term tool ownership.

Bulk & B2B

Pricing is volume-based and quoted per order. Typical tiers: MOQ-999 units (standard), 1,000-4,999 units (5-10% savings), 5,000+ units (custom pricing). Container-load orders receive the most competitive rates. Contact us for a tailored quote.
