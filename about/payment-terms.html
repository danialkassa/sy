PAYMENT & TRADE TERMS

Secure, flexible payment methods and transparent trade terms for smooth international transactions

Discuss Payment Options

Payment Methods

We offer multiple secure payment options to accommodate buyers worldwide, from sample orders to full container loads.

T/T (Telegraphic Transfer)

The most common method for B2B orders. Fast processing with direct bank-to-bank transfer.

Standard Terms

30% deposit, 70% before shipment

L/C (Letter of Credit)

Bank-guaranteed payment for maximum security. Ideal for large orders and new business relationships.

Minimum Order

Orders over $10,000

Western Union

Quick and convenient for small transactions. Funds available within minutes of transfer.

Best For

Sample orders under $1,000

Alibaba Trade Assurance

Full order protection through Alibaba's platform. Payment security and quality guarantee for your peace of mind.

Eligibility

Available for qualified orders

Trade Terms (Incoterms)

We support all major incoterms to match your logistics requirements and risk preferences.

Term Full Name Description Best For
FOB Free On Board Seller delivers goods to the port and loads them onto the vessel. Buyer handles shipping from the port of departure. Experienced importers
CIF Cost, Insurance & Freight Seller covers freight and insurance to the destination port. Buyer handles import clearance and local delivery. First-time buyers
EXW Ex Works Buyer picks up goods at the factory. Seller's responsibility ends when goods are made available at their premises. Own logistics partner
DDP Delivered Duty Paid Seller handles everything — shipping, insurance, customs, and delivery to the buyer's door. Maximum convenience. Hassle-free delivery

All trade terms follow ICC Incoterms 2020. Other terms available upon request — contact us to discuss.

Order Process

A clear, step-by-step process from initial inquiry to delivered goods — with payment milestones at every stage.

Inquiry

Submit your product requirements, quantities, and target price. Our team responds within 24 hours.

2

Quotation

3

4
