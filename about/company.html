Established 1998

25+ Years of Manufacturing Excellence

Ningbo Siyang Tools Co., Ltd. was founded in 1998 in the heart of China's manufacturing hub, Ningbo, Zhejiang Province. What began as a small workshop with a vision has grown into a leading power tool manufacturer serving customers across more than 50 countries.

Our journey has been defined by an unwavering commitment to quality, continuous innovation, and deep understanding of professional users' needs. Today, we operate state-of-the-art facilities spanning 50,000 square meters, equipped with the latest manufacturing technology and staffed by over 500 dedicated professionals.

From precision drills to heavy-duty grinders, every Ningbo Siyang product reflects our dedication to engineering excellence and our promise to deliver tools that professionals can rely on, day after day.
